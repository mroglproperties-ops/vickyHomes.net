FORECLOSED

If you have missed more than three mortgage payments, or your lender has filed a Notice of Default (NOD), you might think the loss of your home is inevitable. Even at this stage, many lenders would rather work out a compromise that would allow you to get back on track with your mortgage than take your home in a foreclosure.

BANKRUPTCY

BANKRUPTCY

Bankruptcy stops foreclosure dead in its tracks. Once you file a bankruptcy petition, federal law requires creditors to cease any and all collection activities.

The law requires your mortgage company and other creditors to work in good faith with you to formulate a reasonable repayment plan or allow selling opportunities so you can get back on track.
