Short Sale Specialists!

A short sale, also known as a pre-foreclosure sale is when you fall behind on your mortgage payments, or the value of your property declines below your mortgage balance and a sale may be an option. With a short sale, you will sell your property for a discounted price acceptable to your lender. Once the property is sold, the lender is paid by receiving the remaining mortgage. A short sale may be the answer to your mortgage problems.

We are staffed with a short sale specialist who negotiates on your behalf and mediates the process of the sale at no cost to you!!!
